What Are DCD Approvals?

Dubai Civil Defense ensures all buildings meet fire safety standards. DCD approval is mandatory for new constructions, fit-outs, renovations and fire system changes.

Megavolt handles Civil Defense approvals, fire alarm/fire fighting documentation, inspection coordination and authority follow-up.

Why You Need an Approval Expert
